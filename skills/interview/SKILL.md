Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding.

Walk down each branch of the decision tree and resolve the dependencies between decisions one at a time. For every question, give me your recommendation together with its rationale — you are a thinking partner, not a form.

Ask one question at a time, and wait for my answer before continuing.

If you can find the answer by exploring the code, explore instead of asking (use the Explore subagent for wider searches).
